Risk Monitoring
Structured Risk Register
Document every identified risk in a structured register — capturing risk type, likelihood, severity, impacted participants or staff, and the mitigation plan in place — so your whole organisation has a clear, shared view of what's being managed and how.
Centralised · Structured · Org-wide
Participant Risk Plans
Build individual risk plans for each participant — identifying their specific vulnerabilities, the supports required to manage them, and the behaviours or situations that require immediate escalation. Support workers see the plan before every shift so they deliver care with full situational awareness.
Per-participant · Shift-visible · Always updated
Mitigation Tracking & Review
Track the status of every mitigation action — assigned, in progress, or completed — and schedule regular risk reviews so plans stay current. When an incident occurs, Plannor automatically links it to the relevant risk entry to strengthen your risk intelligence over time.
Action-tracked · Reviewed regularly · Incident-linked

Risk Tools

All the tools to identify, manage and reduce risk.

Risk registers, participant risk plans, mitigation tracking, and workforce monitoring — everything needed to stay ahead of harm and meet your NDIS duty of care.

Centralised Risk Register
A single register for all participant and workforce risks — rated by likelihood and severity, with mitigation actions assigned and tracked.
Participant Risk Plans
Individual risk plans per participant that support workers view before shifts — covering known vulnerabilities, triggers, and required responses.
Risk Level Alerts
When a participant's risk profile changes — new incident, updated health status, or escalating behaviour — coordinators are automatically alerted to review and update the plan.
Mitigation Action Tracking
Assign mitigation actions to team members, track their status, and get notified when overdue — ensuring risks don't just get documented but actually get managed.
Workforce Risk Monitoring
Identify workforce risks — expired credentials, lone worker situations, overloaded rosters — and address them before they put staff or participants at risk.
Audit-Ready Risk Records
Every risk, plan, review, and action is stored with a full audit trail — ready to demonstrate due diligence to the NDIS Commission at any time.
