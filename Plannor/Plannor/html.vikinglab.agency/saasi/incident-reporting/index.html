Incident Tools

All the tools to manage incidents and stay compliant.

From instant logging and automatic escalation to mandatory reporting tracking and audit-ready records — Plannor handles every step of incident management so nothing falls through the cracks.

Digital Incident Forms
Structured digital forms guide workers through exactly what to record — no blank pages, no missed details, no paper that gets lost before it reaches the office.
Severity Classification
Incidents are automatically classified by severity so the right escalation path is triggered — minor incidents route to supervisors, serious incidents go straight to management and the Commission.
Coordinator Alerts
Coordinators are instantly notified when an incident is logged — with full details, severity level, and the required response timeframe right in the alert.
NDIS Commission Reporting
Track which incidents require mandatory notification to the NDIS Commission, when they're due, and whether they've been submitted — so deadlines are never missed.
Investigation Tracking
Record investigation progress, assign responsible staff, and document outcomes — keeping a complete, timestamped trail from the initial report through to resolution.
Audit-Ready Incident Register
Every incident ever logged is stored in a searchable register — date, participant, type, severity, actions taken, and outcome — ready for any NDIS audit or internal review.
