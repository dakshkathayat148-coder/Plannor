03 Support Workers

Less Paperwork. More Time With Participants.

Support workers are the heart of every NDIS service — but admin gets in the way. Plannor's mobile-first tools are built for workers in the field, not at a desk.

The Challenges
Accessing Information Care plans, goals, medication notes, and handover information spread across binders, emails, and different apps
Completing Notes Writing NDIS-compliant progress notes after every shift is time-consuming, repetitive, and often done hours after the support occurred
Managing Shifts Last-minute schedule changes, unclear handover instructions, and no live view of upcoming shifts create confusion and missed support
 How Plannor Helps
Mobile Participant Profiles

All care plans, goals, risk notes, and handover instructions accessible on your phone — even offline. Workers arrive to every shift fully prepared with exactly the information they need.

Voice-to-Text AI Notes

Speak your progress notes and Plannor's AI structures, formats, and links them to participant goals automatically. NDIS-compliant notes in under 30 seconds — no typing required.

Live Shift Management

See your full schedule, accept or decline shifts, receive real-time updates for changes, and submit handover notes directly from the app. No more missed shifts or communication gaps.

Instant Incident Reporting

Log incidents on the spot from your phone with guided templates. Reports are auto-formatted for NDIS Commission requirements and sent to your coordinator immediately.
