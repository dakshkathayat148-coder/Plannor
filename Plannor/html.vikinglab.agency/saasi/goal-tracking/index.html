Goal Tracking Tools

Everything you need to prove participant progress.

From linking supports to goals and capturing milestone evidence, to generating plan review reports — Plannor keeps every participant's progress organised, up to date, and ready to share.

NDIS Goal Creation
Set up each participant's NDIS goals directly in Plannor — aligned to their plan categories, with custom milestones and measurable targets that everyone on the team can track.
Milestone Sign-Off
Supervisors and coordinators can review and sign off on milestone completions — creating a verified, timestamped record of participant progress that holds up under any NDIS audit.
Worker Goal Notes
Support workers record what occurred each shift and tag it against the relevant goal — capturing real-time evidence that would otherwise be lost by the time review comes around.
Progress Dashboards
Coordinators get a live view of progress across all participants and goals — instantly seeing which goals are on track, which are stalled, and where extra support attention is needed.
Outcome Evidence Reports
Generate a complete outcome evidence report for any participant at any time — pre-populated with goal progress, milestones, support notes, and hours delivered, ready to hand to planners at review.
Participant & Family Visibility
Share goal progress directly with participants and their families — giving them full visibility into what's being worked toward, what's been achieved, and how their funding is making a difference.
