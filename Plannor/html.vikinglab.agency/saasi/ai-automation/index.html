Automation Tools

Everything you need to automate the admin away.

From automated reminders and triggered workflows to hands-free scheduling adjustments — Plannor AI handles the repetitive tasks so your team can focus on what only humans can do.

Document Expiry Reminders
Automatic reminders sent to workers and coordinators before licences, police checks, and first aid certificates expire — no manual tracking, no last-minute scrambles.
Missed Clock-In Triggers
If a support worker doesn't clock in by shift start time, Plannor AI instantly alerts the coordinator — so you can follow up before a participant is left without support.
Agreement Follow-Up Automation
Unsigned service agreements trigger automatic follow-up reminders to participants and their nominees — eliminating the manual chase that delays onboarding and service delivery.
Plan Review Scheduling
Plannor AI automatically schedules plan review reminders ahead of NDIS plan end dates — giving coordinators enough lead time to gather evidence, prepare reports, and book reviews without rushing.
Budget Alert Automation
When a participant's NDIS budget reaches 80%, Plannor AI automatically notifies the support coordinator — triggering a review before funding is exhausted and services are disrupted.
Shift Cancellation Workflows
A cancelled shift automatically triggers a replacement search, notifies available workers, and alerts the coordinator — turning a potential service gap into a managed process handled by AI.
