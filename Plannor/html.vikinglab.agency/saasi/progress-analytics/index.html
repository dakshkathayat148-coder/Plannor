Analytics Tools

Everything you need to track progress and improve outcomes.

From visual participant dashboards and trend detection to service performance breakdowns and exportable analytics reports — Plannor puts the data your team needs to continuously improve right at their fingertips.

Caseload Overview Dashboard
See the health of your entire caseload on one screen — progress rates, active goals, budget utilisation, and support hours — so coordinators can prioritise their day based on data, not instinct.
Progress Trend Charts
Visualise participant progress over time with trend charts that reveal whether momentum is building, plateauing, or declining — giving coordinators the data needed to adjust supports before progress stalls.
Goal Completion Analytics
Track goal achievement rates across individual participants and your entire organisation — identifying which goal types are consistently met and which are regularly falling short so you can adjust how supports are planned.
At-Risk Participant Flags
Plannor automatically flags participants whose progress data suggests they are at risk — stalled goals, declining engagement, reduced support hours — so coordinators can step in early with the right intervention.
Worker Performance Insights
See which support workers are consistently driving the strongest participant progress — enabling targeted training, better rostering decisions, and a culture of continuous improvement across your team.
Exportable Analytics Reports
Export progress analytics reports for individual participants, goal areas, or your whole organisation — formatted for plan reviews, internal meetings, and continuous improvement planning without manual data extraction.
