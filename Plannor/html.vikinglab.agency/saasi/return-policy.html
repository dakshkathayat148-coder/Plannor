Plannor Return Policy

Return Policy

Plannor is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform. Because our product is delivered digitally, the following return and refund terms apply to all subscriptions and purchases made through Plannor.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your Plannor subscription at any time from your account settings or by contacting our support team. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. You will continue to have full access to Plannor until that date — no service is interrupted early.

2. Refunds

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you are not satisfied within the first 14 days of your paid plan, contact us and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

After 14 days, subscription fees are non-refundable. Partial refunds are not provided for unused portions of a billing period.

14-day money-back guarantee applies to all new paid plans.

3. Exceptions

The following purchases are not eligible for a refund:

  • Annual plans cancelled after the 14-day guarantee window.
  • Add-on purchases (e.g. additional user seats, premium modules) once activated.
  • Accounts suspended for violations of our Terms of Service.

4. Billing Errors

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, please contact us within 30 days of the charge. We will investigate and issue a correction or credit if a billing error is confirmed.

5. Free Trials

Free trial periods are not charged. No refund is applicable to a trial period. Upgrading from a trial to a paid plan constitutes acceptance of these return and refund terms.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Return Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of Plannor after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Our support team is here to help with any billing or refund enquiries.
