Roster Optimisation
AI suggests specific roster changes to reduce overtime, minimise travel between visits, and better match workers to participants based on skills, history, and proximity.

All AI recommendations are ranked by urgency and impact — so coordinators and managers know exactly where to focus first, without sifting through dashboards or reports to figure it out themselves.
