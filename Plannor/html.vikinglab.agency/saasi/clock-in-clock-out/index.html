Time Tracking Tools

Everything you need to track shifts with confidence.

From GPS-verified clock ins to automated timesheets and late arrival alerts — Plannor gives your team the tools to manage every shift without the admin overhead.

GPS Location Verification
Confirm workers are physically at the participant's address when they clock in — preventing fraud and giving coordinators proof of attendance for every shift.
Late Arrival Alerts
If a worker hasn't clocked in by shift start time, Plannor automatically alerts the coordinator — so you can follow up immediately and ensure the participant isn't left without support.
Automatic Timesheets
Clock in and out data flows automatically into digital timesheets — accurate to the minute and ready for payroll processing without any manual data entry.
Live Shift Dashboard
See every active shift in real time — who's on, who's late, and who's finished. One view gives coordinators complete visibility across all participants and workers.

Shift hours are automatically calculated in line with SCHADS Award requirements — including overtime, allowances, and penalty rates — feeding directly into payroll.
NDIS Claiming Records
Every clocked shift becomes an audit-ready time record — linked to the participant, support category, and funding line — ready for NDIS claiming and compliance review.
