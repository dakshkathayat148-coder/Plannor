Document Management

All your critical documents in one secure place.

Store, organise, and track every staff and client document with automatic expiry alerts — so nothing slips through the cracks before an audit or inspection.

Availability & Leave

Roster smarter with real-time availability.

Let staff set their own availability and submit leave requests. Plannor automatically flags conflicts when you build the roster — no more double-booking or awkward surprises.

  • Staff-managed availability Workers update their own availability windows, giving coordinators an accurate, up-to-date picture for rostering.
  • Leave request workflow Staff submit leave, managers approve or decline — all tracked with automatic calendar updates and notifications.
  • Conflict detection Plannor flags roster conflicts the moment you schedule someone unavailable or on leave — before it becomes a problem.
  • Leave balance tracking Annual, personal, and sick leave balances are tracked automatically and visible to both staff and managers.
